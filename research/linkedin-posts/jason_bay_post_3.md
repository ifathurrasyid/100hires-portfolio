If you're still using "AI chats" instead of "AI agents"—you're officially getting left behind. 

Chats were all the rage in 2025. 

They relied on prompting. You'd see guides dozens of pages long containing prompts to:

- Do account research
- Write cold emails
- Create messaging
etc.


⛔️ The major cons of only using "AI chats"

• Little built-in memory, relies on heavy prompting
• Completely reactive, only responds when you prompt
• Not great at large, complex tasks


✅ The major pros of "AI agents"

• Knowledge compounds over time: the more you wire in, the more it knows how to help.
• Can run proactively and on autopilot
• Can chain multiple tasks together


This is the future of work. High-level tasks completed without prompts.

Now how it affects the future of the sales org? TBD. I think we're going to see a lot more adoption at the org level. AI agents doing work behind the scenes so that the reps never even have to interact with it.

The best thing you can do is sign up for a Perplexity Computer or Claude Cowork account. And start using it. 

Pay for it out of pocket if you need to.
