Wednesday I'm going live with another piece of the sales rep OS for the AI era.

Personas + Personalities + AI. 

Here's what I've been building:

A workflow that reads a buyer's personality before I ever talk to them.

Before I every talk to someone I feed their LinkedIn profile, posts, and any interviews into Claude and ask for their personality profile. 

After I talk with them, I group all the calls in Otter.ai, connect it to Salesforce and ask it to analyze all my communication with the client and give suggestions on how to adjust my approach and messaging based on their personality. 

No more guessing, generic pitches or wasted communication. 

I'm not teaching theory. I'm showing you the actual system I run on my own deals. The prompts. The workflow. The output.

This is episode 3 of me building the sales rep operating system in public. 

If you've been wondering what it looks like to integrate AI into daily sales without the hype, this is the one.
