Average reply rates right now are what… 1–3%?

Back in 2020, they were significantly higher.

Either way - it’s clear:
reply rates are harder to come by.

AI, spam filters… sure.

But honestly, a lot of the messaging I’m seeing isn’t helping:
→ “your sales team has grown 14%”
→ “noticed you’re hiring an IT leader”
→ “congrats on the recent funding”

It’s all… vanilla.

Surface-level “personalisation” that doesn’t actually mean anything.

Butttt that doesn’t mean we should spend hours researching every account either.

It just means we need to be smarter about what we personalise on.

What’s working for me right now = doubling up triggers.

ZoomInfo makes this easy to find at scale (see image 👇)

The 2 I’m using most:

1) Hiring (but going deeper)
Not just the role - the job description:
→ protecting critical apps
→ ransomware
→ disaster recovery

2) Industry context
Using real examples of what’s happening to similar companies.
